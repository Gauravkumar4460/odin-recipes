Lasagna

Description

lasagna, pasta dish of Italian origin, made with broad often ruffled noodles and a tomato or white sauce.

Lasagna, in the singular, is a southern Italian variation of what northern Italians call lasagne, in the plural.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • pound lean ground
  • cup minced onion
  • cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • cup water
